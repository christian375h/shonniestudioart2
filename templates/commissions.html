COMMISSIONS

Details

My main medium is colored pencil, but I am able to paint acrylic and water color. Through the years I have completed a wide variety of art work. Most drawings can be altered according to your needs. I do require reference photos of what is being drawn.

Please contact Shonnie with any additional questions.

Pricing

  • $100 base price small commission pieces (4" x 6" to 11" x 1 7")
  • $250 base price large commission pieces (12" x 18" to 24" x 36")

Drawings and paintings may vary in price according to the complexity, detail of the work. and hours spent.

Frames may be purchased at extra cost.
